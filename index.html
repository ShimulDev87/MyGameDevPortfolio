About Me

Who I am and what I bring to a team

Hi, I'm Md. Shafi Uddin (Shimul) — a Unity game developer with 4+ years of professional experience building 2D/3D games across mobile, PC, and WebGL. I hold a B.Sc. in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from Southeast University, Dhaka. My passion lies in multiplayer systems, clean game architecture, and shipping polished products that players enjoy.

Multiplayer Systems

Photon PUN2, Photon Fusion 2, Netcode for GameObjects — real-time online games for mobile and PC.

Game Architecture

Mobile Optimization

Android & iOS performance tuning, ad SDK integration (AdMob, Unity Ads, Yodo1 MAS), WebGL builds.

Tools & SDKs

DoTween, Shader Graph, Firebase, PlayFab, Git/GitHub, Puppet Master, Ragdoll physics.
